Gian T. Carlos is a Manila-born musician, DJ, and web developer currently living in Texas. He is a graduate of North Texas with a Bachelor of Science in Fashion Merchandising. He is currently studying Software Engineering at Flatiron School based out of New York City.

He was previously a touring musician and has had DJ residencies at Dallas area clubs like Fallout Lounge, The Cavern, Rubber Gloves, and Mable Peabody. He is a goth at heart with an ongoing love affair with disco. He has a technical mindset with an eye for aesthetics and design.
