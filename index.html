Gian T. Carlos is a Manila-born music curator and web developer currently living in Texas. He has previously held DJ residencies at Dallas and Denton area clubs such as Fallout Lounge, The Cavern, Rubber Gloves, and Mable Peabody's. He is a graduate of North Texas with a Bachelor of Science in Fashion Merchandising. Currently, he is studying Software Engineering at Flatiron School based out of New York City.
